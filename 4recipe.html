Delicious Carrot Halwa

Ingredients

  • 4 cups grated carrots
  • 4 cups milk
  • 4 tbsp ghee
  • ½–¾ cup sugar
  • 4 to 5 cardamoms (powdered)
  • cashews, almonds, raisins

Instructions

  1. 4 Cook grated carrots in milk until soft
  2. Add ghee
  3. Mix sugar
  4. Add cardamoms (powdered)
  5. Add cashews, almonds, raisins
  6. Cook till glossy
